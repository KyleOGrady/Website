Experience

Software Automation Intern; Allscripts

Over the summer of 2017, I was hired as an intern at Allscripts in Burlington Vermont, working in the development department on automated testing. As soon as I began working, I was tasked with extending an automation framework that was still in the beginning stages of development. The purpose of the framework was to run initial smoke-tests on Allscripts' Electronic Health Record, TouchWorks. Over the course of the summer, I worked along side an automation team to write testing scripts in C#, utilizing the Selenium Automation Framework. As a team, we made significant progress on Allscripts' automation framework, and after returning to Clarkson in the fall, I accepted a full time offer to come back and continue working once I had earned my diploma.
